Questions & Answers

Helpful things to know

If there's something we missed, please reach out; we're here to help you plan the perfect trip.

Can I bring a guest?

We would love to celebrate with everyone, but due to space we can only accommodate those listed on your invitation.

What should I wear?

Garden formal: cocktail dresses, dressy jumpsuits, and suits. We'll be outdoors for the ceremony, so stylish flats or block heels recommended.

Are kids welcome?

We adore your families, but the ceremony & reception are adults-only. Nursing infants are welcome; please let us know so we can plan accordingly.

Will there be parking?

Complimentary parking is available at the inn. If you're staying off-site, the shuttle stops at the main downtown hotels.
