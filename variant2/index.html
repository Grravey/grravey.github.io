Questions and answers

A few things friends have already asked.

Can I bring my kids?
We are keeping this dinner adults-only so we can all linger late. Nursing infants are welcome, just give us a heads-up.
What is on the menu?
Family-style dishes inspired by the coast and the forest: cedar-smoked salmon, roasted roots, plenty of seasonal greens, and a surprise dessert.
Is there a registry?
Your presence is all we want. If you insist, there will be a card basket and a honeymoon coffee fund.
Where should I stay?
We have held a few rooms at the Fairview Loft Hotel nearby. Mention the Dahlgren party for a discount or reach out for other ideas.
